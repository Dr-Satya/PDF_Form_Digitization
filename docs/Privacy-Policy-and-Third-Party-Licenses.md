Last updated: 2026-03-08

# 1. Privacy Policy

## 1.1 Roles and scope

The Product supports:

- **Admin users**: authenticated users who create/manage forms and review submissions.
- **App Administrator users**: authenticated superusers who can manage admin user accounts.
- **Public users**: unauthenticated users who access public form links and submit data.

## 1.2 Information we collect

### A) Admin / App Administrator account data

- **Email address**
- **Password hash** (passwords are not stored in plain text)
- **Role** (`admin` / `app_admin`)
- **Timestamps** (e.g., creation time), if enabled in the database schema

### B) Form data (created by Admin users)

- **Uploaded PDFs** (source documents used to generate digital forms)
- **Form schema** (structure/fields used to render the public web form)
- **Public share slug** (random share identifier used in public URLs)

### C) Public form submission data

- **Submitter email address** (mandatory)
- **Field values entered by the submitter**
- **Generated PDF output path** (the filled PDF generated by the system)
- **Submission time**

## 1.3 How we use the information

We use the data to:

- Authenticate and authorize users.
- Allow Admin users to create, publish, and manage forms.
- Allow Public users to submit form responses.
- Generate filled PDFs from submitted data.
- Provide downloads of generated PDFs to authorized Admin users.

## 1.4 Data sharing

- Public submission data and generated PDFs are visible only to the Admin user who owns the form (and to App Administrator users only to the extent required to manage admin accounts).
- We do not sell personal data.

## 1.5 User-submitted content and responsibility

- Content submitted through public forms (including personal data) is submitted by the public user to the Admin user who published the form.
- Admin users are responsible for the content they collect via forms, including providing any notices/consents required by law and configuring their own retention policies.

## 1.6 Data storage

- Application data is stored in a PostgreSQL database.
- Uploaded PDFs and generated PDFs are stored on the server/storage configured for the deployment.

## 1.7 Data retention and deletion

- Retention policies depend on the deployment configuration.
- Admin users can delete:
  - Forms
  - Submissions (including bulk delete)
- Deleting a submission removes it from the Product’s submission records; generated files may also be removed depending on storage and cleanup behavior.

## 1.8 Security

- Authentication is implemented using JWT.
- Passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes.
- Access controls are enforced to restrict Admin users to their own forms/submissions.

Security disclaimer:

- No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we implement reasonable technical measures, we do not guarantee that the Product will be free from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure of data.

## 1.9 Cookies, advertising, and Google AdSense

- The Product may store authentication tokens in local storage in the browser for Admin/App Administrator sessions.
- If enabled by the deployment configuration, the Product may display advertising, including **Google AdSense**.
- Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this or other websites.
- Users may be able to control advertising cookies through their browser settings and/or Google’s ad settings.

## 1.10 Limitation of responsibility for user data

- Public users should not submit sensitive personal information unless they trust the Admin user who published the form.
- Admin users should not upload or collect data they are not authorized to process.
- To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, we disclaim responsibility for user-submitted content and the consequences of Admin users collecting, storing, sharing, or processing such content.

## 1.11 Changes to this policy

We may update this document as the Product evolves. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
